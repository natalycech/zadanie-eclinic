EuroPainClinics University
Introduction
EuroPainClinics University is an international online educational platform under the auspices of EuroPainClinics®,a brand operating a network of European interventional pain clinics.
In a training program for pain management specialist, EuroPainClinics University reflects their many years of high expertise, clinical experience and extensive expert and educational activities. An effective form of teaching is provided by eight profile departments.
Head of University
Róbert Rapčan
M.D.,PhD.,MBA,FIPP
Dr.Róbert Rapčan,PhD.,MBA,FIPP,Medical Director of the EuroPainClinics network, is a leading European specialist in minimally invasive and endoscopic back treatment certified in Sweden, Great, Britain, Slovakia and the Czech Republic.
Dr.Rapčan has for a long time maintained the trend of introducing innovative treatment methods and techniques, special methodologies and the latest medical device technologies into the clinical practice of all EuroPainClinics workplaces.
